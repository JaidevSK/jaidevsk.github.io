Flowers of IITGN

Introduction

Flowers of IITGN is a Web Application developed by PMDCB Lab at IIT Gandhinagar to spread awareness about the huge biodiversity and variety of flower species present in the campus. This application comes with a search by name featue where we can select a flower by its species name. We also have a Gallery which displays the variety of flowers present in the campus. Apart from these, there is also a the feature of ientifying the flower just by its image. And finally we will have a Flower ChatBot, which will always be ready to let us explore more about the Flowers of IITGN.
